Story

A long, long time ago in a swol, swol galaxy... After leaving the sweg planet earth, a group of reapers fly toward a distant speck. The speck gradually resolves into a small, space Dylemma. Civil war strikes the galaxy, which is ruled by Dark Dirk, an angry Leviathan capable of arson and even murder. Terrified, a dank Cthulhu known as General Bobo flees the Empire, with his protector, Dirk Murphy. They head for Mazda on the planet Gliese. When they finally arrive, a fight breaks out. Murphy uses his swol plasma shotgun to defend General. Murphy and Cthulhu General decide it's time to leave Gliese and steal a space ship to shoot their way out. They encounter a tribe of cannibal. Murphy is attacked and the Cthulhu is captured by the cannibal and taken back to Mazda.< Murphy must fight to save Cthulhu General but when he accidentally unearths a dangerous flashlight, the entire future of the swol, sweg galaxy is at stake.
